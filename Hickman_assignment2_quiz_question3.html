    How to Make Boxed Macaroni and Cheese:
  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Open box of macaroni and remove powdered cheese packet
  3. Add noodles to boiling water for 10 minutes
  4. Mix powdered cheese packed with 4 tablespoons of milk
  5. Strain noodles
  6. Mix cheese sauce with noodles
  7. ENJOY!
